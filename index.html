Ice cream Trivia

When did Ice cream first appears in the US?

Ice cream made its debut in the United States in the early 18th century. The first known advertisement for ice cream in America appeared in the New York Gazette on May 12, 1777, placed by confectioner Philip Lenzi, who announced that he was offering ice cream for sale.(Source: Ice Cream for America)
